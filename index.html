About Amaricop

Amaricop Uganda was founded on the belief that “Opportunity” drives personal development and ultimately enhances national development.


Our major objective therefore is to bring opportunities closer to home. We wish for as many Ugandans as possible to have access to education, training and employment opportunities across the world.


While our course of action is currently limited to sourcing for education opportunities for Ugandans and helping a few with their applications, we hope to build our capacity to assist more Ugandans more closely.
